This prototype keeps the full LineUp ranking available, but frames the index through its five pillars first. Use the ranking to sort, group, filter, and inspect language-country pairs.

Communications and Technology Digital support, app accessibility, ICT capacity, and media conditions.
Language Technology Training data, validated hours, articles, users, datasets, and models.
Social and Economic Human development, education, literacy, and economic constraints.
